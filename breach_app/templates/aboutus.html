About BreachTracerX

BreachTracerX is a tool developed by a cybersecurity researcher, python programmer, and digital marketing strategist. It is designed to help you find out if your email has been involved in any data breaches.

Abhishek, at the age of 23, is passionate about cybersecurity and believes in promoting awareness and education in this field. BreachTracerX is currently in the testing phase, and its primary purpose is for educational use only.

Abhishek is the founder of Digistryker Technologies, a company dedicated to providing innovative solutions in the fields of cybersecurity, software development, and digital marketing.

Note: BreachTracerX is strictly for educational purposes. It is not intended for any illegal activities or unethical use. The tool is provided as-is without any warranties. Use it responsibly and respect the privacy and security of others.
